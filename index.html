Gate Walkthrough

Follow these steps in order.

Use Gate, complete the flow, and then return here to use the Links page.

  1. Go to chrome://chrome-signin.

  2. For the email, enter google@d11.org.

  3. Click Sign-in options.

  4. Click Sign in with GitHub.

  5. Scroll to the bottom and click Privacy.

  6. Press Ctrl + F and type ublock.

  7. Click uBlock Origin. This takes you to Wikipedia.

  8. Press Ctrl + F and type google.com site.

  9. Click google.com site.

  10. Search for GitHub, and click on it.

  11. From GitHub, search org:Hpkproject GATE.

  12. Click the link in the README.md (Scroll Down) and open the Gate Exploit Github Link

  13. At the top, click Links to open the sites page and custom redirect.
