# Instructions

These instructions are from the Gate Guide.

1. Go to `chrome://chrome-signin`.
2. For the email, enter `google@d11.org`.
3. Click **Sign-in options**.
4. Click **Sign in with GitHub**.
5. Scroll to the bottom and click **Privacy**.
6. Press **Ctrl + F** and type **ublock**.
7. Click **uBlock Origin**. This takes you to Wikipedia.
8. Search for **google.com**.
9. Press **Ctrl + F** and type **google.com site**.
10. Click **google.com site**.
11. Search for **GitHub**, and click on it.
12. From GitHub, search **org:Hpkproject GATE**.
13. Click the link in the README.md (scroll down) and open the Gate Exploit GitHub link.
14. At the top, click **Links** to open the sites page and custom redirect.
